Q1
⚡ What is AI Project Cycle?
A structured 6-step approach:
Problem Definition → Data Collection → Data Exploration →
AI Modeling → Prediction & Evaluation → Continuous Improvement.
It's a cyclical process that repeats for better results.
Q2
📊 Why is data important?
Quality data is the foundation of accurate AI models.
Good data → reliable predictions & meaningful insights.
Garbage in = Garbage out!
More data + better quality = Better predictions.
Q3
🧠 What is modelling?
Creating a machine learning algorithm that learns
patterns from historical data to make predictions on new data.
Algorithms: Neural Networks, XGBoost, Decision Trees
to identify student stress factors.
Q4
🎯 Why evaluate the model?
Ensures AI is accurate, fair, and reliable before deployment.
Helps identify errors, measure performance,
collect feedback, and make continuous improvements
for better real-world results.
Q5
💙 How can AI reduce student stress?
Early detection of stress patterns · Personalized recommendations ·
Automated support via apps · Timely interventions by counselors ·
Continuous monitoring. AI provides data-driven insights for preventive care.
