The Hot-Dip Galvanizing Advantage

Every Zsharad door is dipped in molten zinc, bonding a protective layer directly to the steel — far stronger than paint or powder coating alone.

  • 25+ years of corrosion resistance
  • Withstands rain, humidity & coastal air
  • No peeling, flaking, or rust bleed
  • Low maintenance, long-term value
  • Eco-friendly & fully recyclable
